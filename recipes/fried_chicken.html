Fried Chicken

Description

Crispy, golden fried chicken with a well-seasoned crust and tender interior.

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the chicken pieces in buttermilk for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large skillet to 350째F (175째C).
  4. Remove chicken from buttermilk, allowing excess to drip off. Dredge each piece in the flour mixture, pressing to adhere.
  5. Fry chicken pieces in batches until golden brown and cooked through, about 12-15 minutes per side. Use a meat thermometer to ensure internal temperature reaches 165째F (74째C).
  6. Drain on paper towels and serve hot.
